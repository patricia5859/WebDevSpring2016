Project - Explore With Me

Link to Project Proposal

Explore with me is a web portal where people can share their love for food and travel. The portal is a social media for food lovers and travellers.


The Food: Users can search for places to eat based on location. The project will call the yelp api to populate the data. Users can also post recipes of food items.


The Travel: A web portal where people can share their travel tours, treks and trips. Users can share their travel plans and also pull in data about travel plans
